# Trackball tracing artefacts
Updated: 2026-02-22
Perhaps small hand/wrist movement while manipulating the trackball would have little to no impact on EEG or even other EXG signals. But because we display a horizontal slider, correlated horizontal eye movement is expected. Let's double check if these expectations are true.
